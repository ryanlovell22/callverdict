Frequently asked questions

What platforms are supported?

CallOutcome works with CallRail and Twilio. You can also upload audio files directly if you use a different platform. Connect your account in under 2 minutes.

How accurate is the AI?

Our AI correctly classifies over 95% of calls. It analyses the full transcript to determine whether a job was booked, not just keywords. You can also manually override any classification.

Can clients see their own calls?

Yes. You can create partner logins for your clients so they can see their assigned calls, or share a proof dashboard link — no login required.

What happens at the call limit?

When you hit your monthly call limit, new calls will queue until your next billing period or until you upgrade. Your existing data is always accessible.

Is there a contract?

No contracts, no lock-in. All plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your billing settings.
